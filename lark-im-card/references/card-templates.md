# Card Templates

Chinese translation: [card-templates.zh-CN.md](card-templates.zh-CN.md)

Reusable examples that can be sent with `lark-cli im +messages-send --msg-type interactive --content '<JSON>'`.

## 1. Simple Notification Card

Title, body, and one action button.

## 2. Multi-Column Info Card

Structured fields for approval or ticket-style data.

## 3. Report Card

Multiple content sections for summaries or daily reports.

## 4. Meeting Card

Meeting title, time, attendees, and agenda.

## 5. Progress Card

Deployment or project progress, often with logs or notes.
